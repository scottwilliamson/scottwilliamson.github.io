FRENCH ENGLISH SONG INDEX

Many songs exist in both French and English. Some are relatively faithful translations, while others may treat completely different topics in the other language.

One language's version may have more lyrics, or primarily the chorus may have migrated to the other language's version of the song. An instrumental song in one culture may have had lyrics written in the other.

This website will take a look at some examples.
